Frequently Asked Questions

How can I request research collaboration?

For research collaboration inquiries, please contact our Research Department at research@arabsustainability.org with details about your proposed collaboration, including objectives, scope, and timeline. Our team will review your request and respond within 5-7 business days.

What are your office hours?

Our offices are open Sunday through Thursday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM (GMT+3). We are closed on Fridays, Saturdays, and public holidays. For urgent matters outside business hours, please call our emergency contact number at +966 50 123 4567.

How do I register for training programs?

You can register for our training programs through our website's Training section, by emailing training@arabsustainability.org, or by calling our Training Department at +966 12 345 6789 ext. 2. Early registration is recommended as our programs often fill quickly.

Where can I access your published reports?

All our published reports are available in the Reports section of our website. Some reports may require free registration to download, while premium reports are available for purchase. For assistance, contact publications@arabsustainability.org.

How can my organization become a partner?

We welcome partnership inquiries from organizations aligned with our mission. Please send your partnership proposal to partnerships@arabsustainability.org, including information about your organization, proposed collaboration areas, and potential mutual benefits. Our Partnerships team will schedule a meeting to discuss opportunities.
