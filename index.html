About the Institute

Leading Sustainability Research in the Arab Region

The Arab Sustainability Institute is a premier research and advisory organization dedicated to advancing sustainable development practices across the Arab world. Founded in 2025, we have established ourselves as a thought leader in environmental, social, and governance (ESG) issues.

Our multidisciplinary team of experts brings together decades of experience in sustainability research, policy development, and corporate consulting. We work closely with governments, businesses, and civil society to develop practical solutions to the region's most pressing sustainability challenges.
