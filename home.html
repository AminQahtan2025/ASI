About the Institute

Founded to foster a greener, more sustainable future in the Arab world, the Arab Sustainability Institute works at the intersection of science, policy, and practice. We believe in knowledge-based transformation through research, education, and consulting services. Our network of experts collaborates globally to create local impact.
