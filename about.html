Leading Sustainability in the Arab World

The Arab Sustainability Institute was founded in 2010 with a vision to accelerate the transition to sustainable development across the Arab region. As an independent research and advisory organization, we bridge the gap between global sustainability knowledge and regional implementation.

Our team of multidisciplinary experts combines international best practices with deep understanding of local contexts, enabling us to develop practical solutions to the region's most pressing environmental, social and governance challenges.

Over the past decade, we have worked with governments, businesses, and civil society organizations across 15 Arab countries, establishing ourselves as a trusted partner for sustainability transformation.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To empower organizations and governments in the Arab world with research-driven solutions that balance economic growth, environmental protection, and social equity.

Our Vision

A thriving Arab region where sustainability principles are integrated into all aspects of development, creating resilient economies and equitable societies.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Collaboration, Innovation, and Regional Relevance guide all our work and relationships.
